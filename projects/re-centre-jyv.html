Recentre Jyväskylä
[10/2021] | [SPIN Unit] [Tampere University]

We brought a network of high-street actors in Jyväskylä together, forming a community around common interest and shared values. We did this by analysing the city spatially, putting the network of actors into touch with experts and each other, and subsequently giving them the tools to deliberate over and ultimately co-create a vision, manifesto, and timeline for future developments. I was in charge of processing and analysing the spatial data, designing the interactive, printed visual material, and creating the community’s website during the workshop.
