AArcade
09/2020 // [AArcade Community]

In May 2019, Damiano and I facilitated MetaNAR as part of the (Re)configuring Territories programme at the Narva Art Residency. The workshop put visual tools — generative algorithims, and image-analysis and GIS software — in the hands of participants. Using the tools, we worked in groups to map facets of the city's digital footprint, broadly conceived. The tools were developed in collaboration with [Rheza Budiono].
