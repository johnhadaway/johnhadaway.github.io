My name is John Holopainen Hadaway. I am broadly interested in how we collaborate and co-exist, in how we live together better. In my life and work, I aim to cultivate, and learn about, coordination, connection, and community.

In practice, I am a generalist, skilled in programming, spatial data analysis and visualisation, design, and writing. At the moment, I am making ourchive, helping develop A Real Space in the heart of Helsinki, and working with SPIN Unit. Prior, I worked in research, strategy, and design, and studied history at the University of Chicago and architecture at the Architectural Association (sort of).

Before all of that, I was born into a rootless, nomadic childhood, lovingly unsettled in Jakarta, Manila, Cebu City, Moscow, and Almaty. I have since lived in Chicago, London, Montpellier, and Helsinki, where I am currently based. In my logged off hours, I like to write, play, rest, and watch Formula 1 cars go vroom vroom.
