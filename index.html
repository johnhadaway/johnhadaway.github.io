My name is John Holopainen Hadaway. I am an aspiring technologist and designer, broadly interested in the ways that we connect, coordinate, and coexist in cities and online.

Currently, I am doing my civil service at the Designmuseo. Previously, I worked in strategic urban research, data visualisation, design, and consulting, received a degree in history from the University of Chicago, and briefly studied architecture at the Architectural Association.

I am Finnish-Filipino-Australian, and currently based in Helsinki — via Chicago, Montpellier, London, Manila, Cebu City, Almaty, Moscow, and Jakarta. In my logged-off hours, I like to write, cook, walk, listen to music, and rest. If any of the above interests you, get in touch via Twitter, Linkedin, or email.
