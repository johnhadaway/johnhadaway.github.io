My name is John Hadaway. I am a technologist and urban planning student. I am motivated, broadly, by built environments that reflect diverse values, and platforms, tools, and data visualisations that help people inhabit — and contribute to — them. At the moment, I am especially interested in collective behaviour and decision-making, urban interfaces and informatics, data visualisation, and local innovation and economic development.

I am Australian, Filipino, and Finnish, and currently based in Helsinki — via Chicago, Montpellier, London, Manila, Cebu City, Almaty, Moscow, and Jakarta. In my spare time, I like to cycle, watch documentaries, listen to music, and read and write. You can reach me via email. I am also on Linkedin and Github.
