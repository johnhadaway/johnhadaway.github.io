Hello! My name is John Holopainen Hadaway. I am a technologist and writer, skilled in frontend development, spatial data analysis and visualisation, design, and strategy. I am interested in coordination mechanisms, social and civic technologies, and cities: what they are and what they can become.

At the moment, I am working on ourchive and resting before my civil service. Previously, I worked in urban analysis and development, research, strategy, and design. I also graduated from the University of Chicago with a degree in history and, for a brief period, studied architecture at the Architectural Association School of Architecture.

Before all of that, I was born into a rootless, nomadic childhood, lovingly unsettled in Jakarta, Manila, Cebu City, Moscow, and Almaty. I have since lived in Chicago, London, Montpellier, and Helsinki, where I am currently based. In my logged off hours, I like to write, play, cook, and rest.
